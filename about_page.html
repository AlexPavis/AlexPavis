Alex Pavis (Geospatial Scientist)

Over the course of my life, I've had many opportunities to travel and experince the world. This prevelidge allowed me to develop a love for world, in both a geographic and a cultural sense. My passion for geography led me to the field of geosptatial science and then to study it at RMIT University.

Over the course of my studies, I've taken many subjects involved in the spatial sciences that are offered at RMIT. The subjects range from learning about different types of geographic data, how to capture and store geographic information and how to present analyses. This has been achieved through subjects such as spatial information science, remote sensing and photogrammetry and cartography.

While at university, I have had two jobs in the spatial science field. For 2.5 years I worked as an assistant surveyor at Prime Survey, a surveying company specialising in new development concrete slab set-outs. Here I was challanged with performing accurate trigenometric calculations and a high-level use of total-station instruments. Since January 2022, I've been working as a geospatial consultant at Tetra Tech Coffey in which I perform geospatial analysis to provide geographical solutions to clients.
